About Me

A little bit of background info about me

My Name is Bryn Winningham, and I am a licensed cosmatologist. Similar to many women, I was tired of putting on maskerra on and dealing with all of that hassel. So, I decided to use my education from Tulsa Tech in cosmatology to start doing something about it. Now, its been about 6 months of lashing full time, its one of my biggest hobbies and is helping me pay for my college education. One of the biggest reasons I chose lashes was to be able to help enhance people's natural beauty so that they can feel better about themselves. I am currently lashing in Stillwater, OK.
